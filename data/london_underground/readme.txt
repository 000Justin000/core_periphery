This .mat file contains the following variables:

Line_Names/Station_Names: These two variables are vectors containing the names of the lines and stations included in the data. There are 14 lines and 317 stations. 

Labelled_Network: This is a 317x317 cell array, where the rows and columns represent the stations, and the entries are vectors containing the line numbers of the lines on which the two stations lie adjacent. 

Tube_Locations: Contains the exact geographical coordinates of each of the stations.
